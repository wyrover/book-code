Author:		of this sound pack: BoogieMan
		of the actual sounds: Blizzard Entertainment

Description:	These are the sounds for the orc model.  They were
		extracted from Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness.

Installation:

	Unzip all the files into a sundirectory called "orc" in your
	players directory.  You can then select the orc in the player
	setup menu in Quake 2 and the sounds will run with it.
